## 📖 Public Domain Publications Included

The following texts are included in this project. All are in the **public domain** under United States copyright law (published before 1928, or their copyright has otherwise expired). **No permission is needed** to reproduce, distribute, or build upon these works.

### The Holy Bible — King James Version (1769)

- **Original Publication:** 1611 (Authorized Version), standardized 1769
- **Status:** Public domain worldwide
- **Included Content:** Complete 66-book Old and New Testament canon
- **You may:** Print, distribute, quote, digitize, and reproduce without restriction

### Strong's Exhaustive Concordance of the Bible

- **Author:** James Strong LL.D., S.T.D. (1822–1894)
- **Original Publication:** 1890
- **Status:** Public domain (U.S.)
- **Included Content:** 8,674 Hebrew/Aramaic entries (H1–H8674), 5,624 Greek entries (G1–G5624)
- **You may:** Reproduce the numbering system, definitions, and transliterations freely

### Easton's Bible Dictionary

- **Author:** Matthew George Easton M.A., D.D. (1823–1894)
- **Original Publication:** 1897 (3rd edition)
- **Status:** Public domain (U.S.)
- **Included Content:** 3,964 entries covering biblical persons, places, doctrines, and customs
- **Data Source:** [solancer/bible-dictionary-scraper](https://github.com/solancer/bible-dictionary-scraper) on GitHub
- **You may:** Reproduce, digitize, and distribute without restriction

### Webster's 1828 American Dictionary of the English Language

- **Author:** Noah Webster (1758–1843)
- **Original Publication:** 1828
- **Status:** Public domain (U.S.)
- **Included Content:** ~61,000 dictionary entries with biblical usage examples
- **You may:** Reproduce and distribute in any format
